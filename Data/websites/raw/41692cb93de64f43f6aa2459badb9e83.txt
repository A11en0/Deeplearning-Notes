        <article class="markdown-body entry-content p-5" itemprop="text"><h1><a id="user-content-xkcd-font" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#xkcd-font"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>xkcd-font</h1>
<p>Fonts derived from the handwriting of @randallmunroe, the <a href="https://xkcd.com" rel="nofollow">xkcd</a> webcomic author.
Yes, it really is his handwriting, and he hopes we <a href="http://xkcd.com/1015/" rel="nofollow">fix the pesky kerning</a>:</p>
<p><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/925be828f20ae2fa17fc29f72dbcc2985adb3c4f/68747470733a2f2f696d67732e786b63642e636f6d2f636f6d6963732f6b65726e696e672e706e67"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/925be828f20ae2fa17fc29f72dbcc2985adb3c4f/68747470733a2f2f696d67732e786b63642e636f6d2f636f6d6963732f6b65726e696e672e706e67" alt="I have never been as self-conscious about my handwriting as when I was inking in the caption for this comic." data-canonical-src="https://imgs.xkcd.com/comics/kerning.png" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
[Credit to xkcd]</p>
<p>This repository contains two fonts, <code>xkcd Script</code> and <code>xkcd</code>, each with their own characteristics (and limitations):</p>
<h3><a id="user-content-font-xkcd-script" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#font-xkcd-script"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Font: <code>xkcd Script</code></h3>
<p><code>xkcd Script</code> is a font derived from a <a href="/ipython/xkcd-font/blob/master/xkcd-script/generator/handwriting_minimal.png">handwriting sample</a> provided by Randall.
It is far less uniform than <code>xkcd</code>, and we think it is therefore more like a true script font.</p>
<p><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="/ipython/xkcd-font/blob/master/xkcd-script/samples/ipsum.png"><img src="/ipython/xkcd-font/raw/master/xkcd-script/samples/ipsum.png" alt="Sample of xkcd-script" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
<p>You can see the font as a <a href="https://cdn.rawgit.com/ipython/xkcd-font/master/preview.html" rel="nofollow">live preview</a>, or
for more information about the font and how it is constructed see the <a href="/ipython/xkcd-font/blob/master/xkcd-script/README.md">xkcd-script/README</a>.</p>
<h3><a id="user-content-font-xkcd" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#font-xkcd"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Font: <code>xkcd</code></h3>
<p>The <code>xkcd</code> font was originally created by Randall, and was used in <a href="https://xkcd.com/1227/" rel="nofollow">xkcd "The Pace of Modern Life" (April 1st, 2013)</a>.
It is considerably more uniform than <code>xkcd Script</code>, which can result in more legibility at the cost of being slightly less like the actual xkcd comic.</p>
<p><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="/ipython/xkcd-font/blob/master/xkcd/samples/ipsum.png"><img src="/ipython/xkcd-font/raw/master/xkcd/samples/ipsum.png" alt="Sample of xkcd" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
