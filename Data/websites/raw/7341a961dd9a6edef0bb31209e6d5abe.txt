  <h1>XMPP in all languages! 03 Dec 2019</h1>
  <p>Welcome to the XMPP newsletter covering the month of November 2019.</p>
<p>Help us sustain this as a community effort, of which <a href="https://wiki.xmpp.org/web/News_and_Articles_for_the_next_XMPP_Newsletter">process is fully documented</a>.</p>
<h2>Articles</h2>
<p>Edivaldo Brito has written in Portugese "<a href="https://www.edivaldobrito.com.br/cliente-jabber-xmpp-dino-no-linux/">Como instalar o moderno cliente Jabber/XMPP Dino no Linux via Snap</a>" ("How to install the modern Jabber/XMPP Dino client on Linux via Snap").</p>
<p><img alt="Dino" src="https://framapic.org/OewIIui3hyiP/UiRW9IhRFoBF.png" /></p>

<h2>Extensions and specifications</h2>
<p>This month, nothing in Last Call, Proposed, Obsoleted.</p>
<h4>Message Retraction</h4>
<p>Version 0.1.0 of XEP-0424 (Message Retraction) has been released.</p>
<p>Abstract:
This specification defines a method for indicating that a message
should be retracted.</p>
<p>Changelog:
Accepted by vote of Council on 2019-10-23. (XEP Editor (jcb))</p>
<p>URL: https://xmpp.org/extensions/xep-0424.html</p>
<h4>Message Moderation</h4>
<p>Version 0.1.0 of XEP-0425 (Message Moderation) has been released.</p>
<p>Abstract:
This specification defines a method for groupchat moderators to
moderate messages.</p>
<p>Changelog:
Accepted by vote of Council on 2019-10-16. (XEP Editor (jcb))</p>
<p>URL: https://xmpp.org/extensions/xep-0425.html</p>
